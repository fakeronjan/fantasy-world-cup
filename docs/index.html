Pick your World Cup

Build a 12-pick squad of teams and players within a $60 salary cap. Score points every match-day as your picks win games, score goals, and advance through the bracket. Trade between rounds. Most points after the Final wins the league.

STEP 1

Draft your roster

Mix teams and players up to a $60 cap, with a max of 12 picks.

e.g. France ($12) + Mbappé ($9) + Brazil ($9) + 9 cheaper picks.

STEP 2

Score all tournament long

Teams earn for wins, draws, and reaching each knockout round. Players earn for goals, assists, clean sheets, and playing in winning matches.

STEP 3

Trade between rounds

After group stage and each knockout round, a transfer window opens. Sell underperformers, buy in on hot picks. Eliminated picks score 0 going forward.

STEP 4

Win the bragging rights

Live leaderboard updates after every match. Most points when the Final whistle blows takes the crown.
